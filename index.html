Mark Zuckerberg

American Computer Programmer and Entrepreneur

Here's the Timeline of Mark Zuckerberg's Facebook journey

  • 1984 - Born in White Plains, New York, U.S
  • 2002 - Enrolled at Harvard University
  • 2004 - Launched facebook.com
  • 2005 - Renamed Facebook
  • 2005 - Facebook received its first major infusion of venture capital ($12.7 million)
  • 2006 - Anyone with an e-mail address could join
  • 2006 - Zuckerberg turned down a $1 billion buyout offer from Yahoo!
  • 2007 - Facebook struck a deal with Microsoft in which the software company paid $240 million for a 1.6 percent stake in Facebook.
  • 2008 - Zuckerberg's new worth was estimated at about $1.5 billion.
  • 2012 - Zuckerberg's net worth was estimated at more than $19 billion.
  • 2049 - Mark Zuckerberg's Death.
